🌟🌟🌟/5

I went to Fogo de Ch達o with my boyfriend for Valentine's Day this year and we opted to get the full steak-focused churrasco dining experience! For those unaware, Brazilian Steakhouses such as Fogo de Ch達o have a unique dining dining experience in which employees walk around with sticks of meat and cut slices of various meat on your command. + Decent quality of meat with my favorite being the Picanha
+ Excellent selection of sides which included P達o de queijo (known as "cheese bread"), selections of cured meats, salad bar, mashed potatoes, and a Market Place table (be careful though, it takes away from how much meat you can eat!

Memory Look Cafe
📌 Downtown Los Angeles

🌟🌟🌟🌟/5

I went to Memory Look Cafe after seeing it all over TikTok! I really enjoy trying new cafes and getting matcha lattes. I also like my drinks really creamy so I enjoyed the selection of eispanner lattes, lattes with a cream foam on top. The outside and inside aesthetic was really nice as well. The drink was perfectly balanced with a good matcha flavour. Parking can be very difficult in Downtown LA but I would definitely come again!

Pros:

+ Great if you like your drinks creamy, sweet, but still with great flavour
+ Lots of room to socialize with friends or get work done
+ Close to restaurants, USC, and other things to do

Cons:

- There's a parking lot but I've experienced times where the security guards haven't let us park there. Surrounding parking is available but more of a hassle
- Drinks are on the pricier side, ~$7.50 + tax for einspanner
- Can be busy on the weekends with long lines!
